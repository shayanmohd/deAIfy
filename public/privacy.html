Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

deAIfy is a free tool that cleans AI writing tells out of your text. The short version: the app has no servers of its own and collects nothing about you.

We do not collect your data

deAIfy has no backend and no analytics. The developer does not collect, store, receive, sell, or share any personal information, usage data, or the text you enter. There are no accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no telemetry.

Free cleanup runs entirely on your device

The default cleanup (removing em dashes, curly quotes, hidden characters, and filler words) runs fully on your device and offline. The text you paste is processed locally and never leaves your device.

Optional AI rewrite mode uses your own key

deAIfy has an optional Pro mode that rewrites text with a large language model. It is off by default and only works if you add your own API key for a third-party provider (Anthropic or OpenAI).

When, and only when, you choose to rewrite text in Pro mode, that text is sent directly from your device to the provider you selected, using your own key. The developer of deAIfy is not in the middle of this request and never receives, sees, logs, or stores your text or your key. That request is governed by the chosen provider's own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Your API key is stored only on your device

If you add an API key, it is stored locally on your device only: on Android in the encrypted Android Keystore, and on the web in your own browser's storage. Your key is never transmitted to the developer. You can delete it any time from the app's Settings screen.

Permissions

The app uses internet access only for the optional Pro mode (to reach the AI provider you chose) and, on the web, to load the app itself. The free cleanup needs no network.

Children's privacy

deAIfy does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page and the date above will be revised.
