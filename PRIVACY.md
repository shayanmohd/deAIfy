# Privacy Policy for deAIfy

**Last updated: 6 July 2026**

deAIfy ("the app") is a free tool that cleans AI writing tells out of your text. This policy
explains what happens to your data. The short version: the app has no servers of its own and
collects nothing about you.

## We do not collect your data

deAIfy has **no backend and no analytics**. The developer does not collect, store, receive,
sell, or share any personal information, usage data, or the text you enter. There are no
accounts, no ads, no trackers, and no telemetry.

## Free cleanup runs entirely on your device

The default cleanup (removing em dashes, curly quotes, hidden characters, and filler words)
runs **fully on your device and offline**. The text you paste is processed locally and never
leaves your device. Nothing is uploaded.

## Optional "AI rewrite" mode uses your own key

deAIfy has an optional Pro mode that rewrites text with a large language model. This mode is
**off by default** and only works if you add your own API key for a third-party provider
(Anthropic or OpenAI).

When, and only when, you choose to rewrite a piece of text in Pro mode:

- The text you are rewriting is sent **directly from your device to the provider you selected**
  (Anthropic or OpenAI), using your own API key.
- The developer of deAIfy is **not** in the middle of this request. We never receive, see, log,
  or store your text or your key.
- That request is governed by the chosen provider's own privacy policy:
  - Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  - OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

## Your API key is stored only on your device

If you add an API key, it is stored **locally on your device only**:

- On Android, in the encrypted Android Keystore.
- On the web, in your own browser's storage.

Your key is never transmitted to the developer. You can delete it at any time from the app's
Settings screen.

## Permissions

The app requests internet access **only** for the optional Pro mode (to reach the AI provider
you chose) and, on the web, to load the app itself. The free cleanup works with no network.

## Children's privacy

deAIfy does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page and the date above will
be revised.
